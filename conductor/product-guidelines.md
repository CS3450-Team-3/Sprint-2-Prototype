# Product Guidelines: CodePop

## Prose Style & Communication
*   **Tone:** Professional and Formal. All documentation, code comments, and user-facing copy should be clear, concise, and technically accurate.
*   **Clarity over Cleverness:** Prioritize unambiguous language to ensure all team members and users understand the intended message.
*   **Standard Terminology:** Use consistent terms for project components (e.g., "Syrup", "Add-In", "Mixologist").

## User Experience (UX) Principles
*   **Speed & Simplicity:** The application must be highly responsive. User flows, especially the "Custom Drink Builder" and "Checkout," should be streamlined to minimize friction and cognitive load.
*   **Engagement & Delight:** Incorporate subtle, high-quality visual feedback and smooth transitions to create an enjoyable and interactive experience that rewards exploration.
*   **Accessibility:** Ensure the application is usable by everyone by following standard accessibility guidelines (e.g., proper contrast, readable font sizes, and support for screen readers).

## Design Philosophy
*   **Vibrant & Modern:** While the prose is formal, the visual design should be modern and vibrant, reflecting the fun and customizable nature of the product.
*   **Consistency:** Maintain a unified look and feel across both the mobile app and any administrative interfaces.
